HOW TO USE:

Press "RANDOMIZE WORLD" to generate a basic planet.
By default the planet will generate in FAST mode.

Hold Right Mouse Button and drag across the screen to Zoom / Move around the 3D scene.

Press "HD" to render the planet in higher definition quality. - CAUTION: MAY CAUSE LAG ON LOWER END DEVICES.

You can mute the music on the top left button of the application.

Adjust parameter accordingly to generate the planet with the sliders.
